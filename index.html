Act Now: Critical Window Closing

Your local MP's support is crucial. We need to maximise support before the Petition Presentation on Monday. Please send your email by Monday, 24th November, 10:00 AM.

Why the deadline? Emails received before Monday morning will be logged by staff and used to update the final, powerful number of constituent supporters briefed to Mr. Zappia immediately before the Petition is formally presented.

The Member for Makin, Tony Zappia, is formally presenting a successful Petition in Federal Parliament, which calls for an investigation into national legislation to reduce light pollution. Your action is required immediately to secure your local MP's support for this vital step.

This historic moment, driven by the Australasian Dark Sky Alliance (ADSA), is the direct result of a national petition that gathered more than 12,000 signatures — a clear signal that Australians care deeply about protecting the night environment.
